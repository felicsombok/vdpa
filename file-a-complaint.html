Important Information – Please Read First

This form is for reporting potential violations of the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA). The Office of the Attorney General of Virginia is the sole enforcement authority for these violations.

We do not offer legal advice or represent individuals in court. Do not submit original documents or personal financial information unrelated to your complaint.

Complaints may be forwarded to other appropriate agencies. Responses may be subject to public disclosure under FOIA. You may request to exclude your contact info from FOIA responses.
