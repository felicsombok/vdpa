Important Information – Please Read First

This form is for reporting potential violations of the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA). The Office of the Attorney General of Virginia is the sole enforcement authority.

We do not offer legal advice or represent individuals in court. Do not submit original documents or personal financial information unrelated to your complaint. Redact Social Security Numbers or unrelated details before uploading.

Complaints may be forwarded to other appropriate agencies. Responses may be subject to public disclosure under the Virginia Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).
